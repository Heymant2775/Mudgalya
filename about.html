ABOUT US

Mudgalya Metallurgy Company, established in 2021, is a forward-looking Indian startup dedicated to manufacturing high-quality precious metal chemicals for the electroplating industry. We specialize in developing and supplying innovative, reliable, and eco-conscious solutions that meet the stringent demands of surface finishing and plating applications. Our focus on quality, customer-centric approach, and commitment to supporting India's manufacturing ecosystem has helped us serve a growing base of industrial clients across the country. At Mudgalya Metallurgy, we aim to drive progress in metallurgical chemistry while contributing to the Make in India vision
